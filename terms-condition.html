Terms & Conditions

Please review our policies carefully before participating in any live breaks, purchases, or community activities on Motrador.

Refund Policy

All sales are final once a group break has been completed. Due to the nature of sports card products, sealed boxes cannot be returned once opened. Because of this, refunds are not available after a break has occurred.

Before purchasing any break spot or product, please carefully review:

  • Break details
  • Product descriptions
  • Team formats
  • Shipping information
  • Break schedules

By completing a purchase, you acknowledge and accept these terms.

Shipping Policy

All orders are shipped to the address entered during checkout.

To ensure secure payment processing and order verification:

  • Orders must ship to the submitted billing/shipping address
  • Customers are responsible for keeping account information updated
  • Address changes should be updated before checkout completion

Weekly Shipping Schedule

Motrador ships orders weekly to provide:

  • Consistent delivery timing
  • Improved packaging efficiency
  • Safer shipment handling
  • Reduced shipping errors

Tracking information will be sent to the email associated with your account once your order has been processed.

What Cards Ship?

Unless otherwise stated in the break listing, shipped cards include:

  • Autographs
  • Relics
  • Serial Numbered Cards
  • Inserts
  • Rookie Cards
  • Major Hits

Some premium products may also include base cards.

Any non-shipped base cards may be donated to:

  • Schools
  • Youth programs
  • Community organizations
  • Hospitals

Community Guidelines

Motrador is built for collectors who share a passion for sports cards and the hobby community.

We expect all members to demonstrate:

  • Respect
  • Honesty

  • Positive community behavior

Harassment, abuse, scams, hate speech, or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated. Any member violating community standards may have their account suspended or permanently removed.
