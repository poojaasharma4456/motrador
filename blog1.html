TURN YOUR #BIGHITTIES INTO CASH WITH MOTRADOR

Fresh off pulling a massive #BIGHITTIE during a live break? Motrador consignment helps you sell premium sports cards quickly, securely, and without the hassle.

Category: #BIGHITTIES Sports Card Consignment Collector Guide

Sitting on a valuable sports card and not sure how to sell it? Maybe you have tried marketplaces before but did not have enough visibility, trusted buyers, or seller protection. For many collectors, handling listings, auctions, messaging, shipping, and after-sale issues takes more time than it is worth.

Motrador makes sports card consignment simple, secure, and hassle-free. As part of the Motrador community, collectors can turn premium cards into cash without dealing with the stress of listing management and buyer communication.

What Cards Qualify for Consignment?

Any sports card valued at $50 or more may qualify. This includes rookie cards, graded cards, autographs, patch cards, low-numbered parallels, rare inserts, and premium hobby hits.

Your card does not need to come from a Motrador break. Many collectors send high-value cards directly from personal collections for consignment services.

How to Submit a Card

Submitting a card is straightforward. Contact the Motrador consignment team and provide:

  • Clear front and back photos of the card
  • Player and product details
  • Grading information (if applicable)
  • Your estimated value

This allows the team to review your submission quickly and recommend the best listing approach.

Maximum Exposure for Better Sale Results

Consignment cards are professionally listed through trusted marketplace channels using auction-style listings to increase competition among buyers. Motrador's collector community and marketplace audience help drive stronger visibility for premium cards.

Track Listings and Sales with Confidence

Collectors can track consignment cards through listing descriptions connected to account or username information. This makes it easy to monitor active listings, watch auction progress, and review completed sales.

Transparent Process and Flexible Payouts

Motrador keeps the process transparent and straightforward. Standard consignment fees apply after marketplace and payment processing fees. Collectors can choose flexible payout options, including direct payouts or store credit toward future breaks and hobby purchases.

Trust Matters in Sports Card Consignment

Sellers want confidence that cards are handled professionally. Buyers want assurance that products are authentic and secure. Motrador supports both by managing listing operations, buyer communication, secure payments, shipping logistics, protective packaging, and post-sale support.

Once the seller receives payment, Motrador continues managing buyer-related concerns including shipping support, returns, and customer service. That removes stress from the seller and creates a smoother overall experience.
